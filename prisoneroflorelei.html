Prisoner of Lorelei

Dream of morrow, thy sorrow, I borrow

A morrow that never doth come, vile gloom, that loom

Thy bewitching voice beguiled me, shalt be, never flee

Waylaid and astray, far away, my Bey

Lass, even Kenning full well.
